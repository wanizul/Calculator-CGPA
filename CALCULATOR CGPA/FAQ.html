frequently asked questions

How long is the duration of study for studies a programme?

Our degree consists of 4 years, Master programme is 2 years and Phd programme is 3 years.

Is there any age limit to apply for Undergraduate programmes? What about application to Postgraduate studies?

According to the Malaysia Immigration department, a student must not exceed the age of 21 years old to be offered for undergraduate programmes, and there is no age limit for postgraduate programmes.

What is English Proficiency Test (EPT)?

The English Proficiency Test (EPT) must be taken by those offered without TOEFL or IELTS that meets the requirement for exemption of English courses.

What is a processing fee?

Processing fee is a sum (USD$100) of payment to be paid (non-refundable) for processing the application.

aWhat is the minimum requirement?

We accept Advanced Level (A level) result only with a minimum of 3 subjects or any other HSC results. Advanced Subsidiary and Ordinary Level (O level) results are not acceptable for application into undergraduate programmes. Postgraduate requires minimun a of CGPA 2.75.

How do I know if I am offered?

You will receive an automated notification email and you may login to your online application account to check your offer letter for admission to the SANZ undergraduate/postgraduate programme.
